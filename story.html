Our Story

1988

The first Fried Chicken restaurant is founded by Martin Feather, who starts the business in a small storefront in downtown.
Feathers N' Fries quickly becomes a popular spot for locals looking for a quick and satisfying meal.

1990

Feathers N' Fries expands to a second location, offering even more delicious fried chicken options for customers.
The new location is in the suburbs, giving more people access to Feathers N' Fries's famous chicken.

1991

Feathers N' Fries introduces a new line of sandwiches and salads, catering to customers looking for healthier options.
The new menu items are a hit, and Feathers N' Fries starts to see an increase in lunchtime traffic.

1993

Feathers N' Fries begins to franchise, opening locations in other cities across the country.
The first franchise opens in Phoenix, Arizona, and quickly becomes a favorite among locals.

1994

Feathers N' Fries introduces a new line of side dishes, including macaroni and cheese and coleslaw.
These new options are well-received and help to round out Feathers N' Fries's menu.

1996

Feathers N' Fries launches a new marketing campaign, emphasizing the quality and freshness of its ingredients.
The campaign helps to attract new customers and boost sales.

1999

Feathers N' Fries introduces a new line of sauces and marinades, allowing customers to customize their meals to their tastes.
These new options are a hit and help to drive sales.

2001

Feathers N' Fries begins to offer online ordering and delivery options for customers.
This new service is well-received and helps to increase convenience for customers.

2002

Feathers N' Fries launches a new loyalty program, offering rewards and discounts to frequent customers.
This program helps to build customer loyalty and boost sales.

2006

Feathers N' Fries introduces a new line of desserts, including peach cobbler and sweet potato pie.
These new options help to round out Feathers N' Fries' menu and attract customers looking for a sweet treat.

2008

Feathers N' Fries opens its first drive-thru location, making it even more convenient for customers.
This new location helps to increase sales and attract new customers.

2009

Feathers N' Fries launches a new mobile app, allowing customers to easily order and pay for their meals.
This app helps to increase convenience for customers and boost sales.

2011

Feathers N' Fries begins to offer catering services, providing delicious fried chicken for events and parties.
This new service is well-received and helps to increase revenue.

2012

Feathers N' Fries introduces a new line of breakfast items, including chicken and waffles.
These new options are a hit and help to increase traffic in the morning.

2013

Feathers N' Fries expands to a new, larger location, allowing for even more customers to enjoy its delicious food.
The new location is in a prime spot and helps to attract new customers.

2014

Feathers N' Fries begins to offer a meal-plan service, delivering pre-made meals to customers' homes.
This new service is well-received and helps to increase convenience for customers.

2018

Feathers N' Fries introduces a new line of vegetarian options, catering to customers with different dietary needs.
These new options are well-received and help to attract new customers.
